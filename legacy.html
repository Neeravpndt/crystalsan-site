Our Legacy

A proud journey of trust, quality, and excellence in sanitary & plumbing solutions.

Who We Are

CrystalSan is a premium brand in sanitary and plumbing products, founded with the vision to deliver unmatched quality and long-lasting performance. With decades of experience in the industry, we have become a trusted name across northern India, especially in UP and Uttarakhand. Our legacy is built on strong partnerships, consistent supply, and attention to every detail our customers care about.

Milestones

1998

Humble Beginnings

Started as a local distributor of sanitary fittings in western UP.

2008

CrystalSan Brand Launch

Established our own premium product line, branded as CrystalSan.

2015

Expansion Across States

Started distribution in Uttarakhand and eastern UP.
